Personal Coaching Sessions

Training sessions will focus on the areas you want to improve the most

Practising close control ball skills will improve your touch and help you stay composed when under pressure. Using carefully designed drills that focus on using both feet and both sides of the foot will enhance your adaptability as a player.

Working on fitness and strength will improve your endurance and ability to hold up the ball in play. Sessions can be developed to focus more on speed and agility or core strength and fitness.

Combination drills are an excellent way to combine skill work and fitness. These can be structured to include possesion, control, passing and shooting skills and aim to test these skills under pressure and with tierd legs.

About Me

Over 5 years coaching experience of all abilities

I started coaching straight from college where I studied sports coaching with development of sport. I havent always stuck in with all sports but football, football has always been my go to. I always wanted to better myself, so I would make drills up at the park, down a bit of grass out my back where ever. Just as long as you have a ball. That's the best part about making football happen and bringing people together. All you need is a ball.

From playing football out in the streets with my mates to now playing for United Glasgow a finalcially and socially inclusive football team, which I sit in the footbalIing commitee for and have started training new community coaches to help with our volunteer based team. 4 years ago I started helping out at drop - in sessions for women in glasgow through United. Whilst running these sessions for the last 3 years every week I wanted to provide that wee bit more. I wanted to provide more community fun foootball! So I secured funding to run 7s for women in glasgow with free child care, kit and boots for women on the day.

All of this has given me the confidence to take second training sessions for my 11s team, to making my own drills, setting up training fitness programs for them and get more football provided for the people that want and need it.

Now finally I have taken my passion and turned it into a buiness i.e. Porteous 121 football coaching! From 5+ years of coaching to this. I want to provide football for all, all abilties, all genders just all people really. Using this to give football, fitness but also a safe place to confidently learn new skills on the ball and feel better for it.
